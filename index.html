About

AuricTime watches are crafted for those who appreciate the synergy between impeccable craftsmanship and timeless design. Each piece is an emblem of luxury, designed to accentuate the sophistication of its wearer.

  • Swiss Movement: Renowned for its precision and durability.
  • Sapphire Crystal Glass: Scratch-resistant and anti-reflective coating for optimal clarity.
  • 18K Gold Finishing: Adds a touch of opulence and ensures lasting elegance.
  • Water Resistance: Up to 100 meters, perfect for both adventure and everyday elegance.
  • Limited Edition Series: Each model is part of a exclusive collection, enhancing its uniqueness and investment value.
